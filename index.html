QRGen Pro supports all standard QR code types including URL, text, email, phone, SMS, vCard, WiFi credentials, and more. Our platform also supports dynamic QR codes that can be edited after creation without changing the QR code image.

Dynamic QR codes contain a short URL that redirects to your final destination. This allows you to change the destination URL at any time without needing to reprint or redistribute the QR code. You can also track scans in real-time and gather analytics about when and where your QR codes are being scanned.

You can download your QR codes in multiple formats including PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, and EPS. Vector formats (SVG, EPS) are perfect for printing at any size without quality loss, while raster formats (PNG, JPG) are ideal for digital use.
